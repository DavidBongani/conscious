ADVANCING THE WORLD THROUGH TECHNOLOGY

We are forward thinkers, driven by our passion to continuously innovate smarter solutions and enable important shifts.

PURPOSE AND STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

Our role is clear – through every action, every day, we make the shift and advance the world through technology.

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

Digital Shift

Our objective is to be an industry leader in digital solutions. We will grow our digital customer offering to facilitate both big and small business. Internally, we will ensure efficiency through a seamless flow, supported by standardized business systems across our sites.

Sustainability Shift

We have development sustainability goals in the areas of climate, circularity, people and fair play that will be achieved by building products, platforms, and services that help people and businesses grow. All business areas and divisions have plans and actions to deliver on the goals and we will transparently report on the progress.

Customers First Choice

Across the organization we want to advance the way we measure and improve customer value and customer satisfaction. Our divisions will be able to track their customer satisfaction improvements through structured methods and show continuous measurable progress.

COMPANY DIVISIONS

WEB DEVELOPMENT DIVISION

Driven by best industrial practice, we build web pages that load fast, expose the desired service, and are comfortable to view on all devices: from a desktop computers to tablets and mobile phones. We implement modern web pages with HTML and CSS and code our pages such that its components rearrange and resize themselves automatically based on the size of the user’s screen. We deliver web pages that will be just as useful on a mobile phone as on a desktop computer. Using Javascript, we build a fully functional web application that utilizes Ajax to expose server-side functionality and data to the end user.

DATA ANALYTICS DIVISION

We analyse data to help businesses improve their processes, identify opportunities and trends, launch new products, and make thoughtful decisions. We accomplish common data analyst tasks with the best tools and resources. Our professional toolbox consists of key analytical skills (data cleaning, data analysis, data visualization) and tools (spreadsheets, SQL, Python, R programming, Tableau).

PROJECT MANAGEMENT DIVISION

Helping businesses by appling our knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to project activities to meet the project requirements. Our project managers ensure projects within an organization are managed and completed with maximum value and benefits realized. We create effective project documentation and artifacts throughout the various phases of a project using software such as Agile project management, with a focus on implementing Scrum events, building Scrum artifacts. We also ensure strategic communication, problem-solving, and stakeholder management.

IT SUPPORT DIVISION

Businesses, nonprofits, school systems, and government organizations all rely on technology. Computers break, systems fail, phones die—when that happens, we are the experts to fix them. IT support is all about troubleshooting and problem solving, and providing great customer service along the way. This includes setting up computers for users within an organization, overseeing computer systems, and debugging whenever a problem arises. we offer IT solutions in-person or remote help desk work in a range of organizations across many different industries.

IT AUTOMATION WITH PYTHON

The world of IT is changing rapidly and we strive to implement latest technologies. We write code to solve problems and to automate solutions. Tasks are written in Python scripts for automation. Git and GitHub are used for version control. We manage IT resources at scale, both for physical machines and virtual machines in the cloud.

Conscious is an innovative solutions group in web development, software engineering, internet technology, data analysis and project management.
